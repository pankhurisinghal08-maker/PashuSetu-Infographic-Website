Empowering rural India by providing instant access to veterinary care and livestock commerce.

Why PashuSetu?

The Challenge

  • Lack of immediate veterinary access in rural areas.
  • Exploitative middlemen in livestock trading.
  • Misinformation regarding animal health and diet.

The PashuSetu Solution

  • Telemedicine: Video call expert vets instantly.
  • Marketplace: Buy/Sell cattle directly without commission.
  • Community: Verified forums for health tips.
